Who We Are

A Family That Builds

Nalkudil means "Good Home" in Tamil — and that's exactly what we're building. A studio rooted in family values, creating apps that make everyday life a little more meaningful.

We're Sarav & Vidhya, a husband-and-wife team from Chennai, India. Parents to Dhruv (8) and Raashvi (2). We started Nalkudil Studio to build products that matter — for couples, families, friends, and creators. While Vidhya does the vibe coding and build app, we together brainstorm what problem to solve.

Our goal is simple: build purposeful apps, publishing, and community — one meaningful product at a time.
