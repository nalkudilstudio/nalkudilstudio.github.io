Who We Are
A Family That Builds

Nalkudil means "Good Home" in Tamil — and that's exactly what we're building. A studio rooted in family values, creating apps that make everyday life a little more meaningful.

We're Sarav & Vidhya, a husband-and-wife team from Chennai, India. Parents to Dhruv (8) and Raashvi (2). While Vidhya does the vibe coding, we together brainstorm what problems to solve. Our goal is simple: build purposeful apps that earn passive income while genuinely helping people.
